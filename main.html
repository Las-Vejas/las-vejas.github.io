Starting out

To start out your smart home setup you have to choose a platform to start out with. You can choose between different platforms from an Apple centered one to a Google centered one. You have a few options to choose from, like:

-Apple Home (Previously known as HomeKit)

-Matter (Not really a platform)

-Google Home

-Amazon Alexa

-Home Assistant
