What is Matter?

Matter is practically like a language that lets all the rival ecosystems like HomeKit and Google Home to talk to each other. Matter lets you control the same devices simultaneously.

Matter is super fast to update between devices and ecosystems because it runs on Thread and Wifi. Theard is a mesh network that lets accesories talk to each other which allows for a super fast connection.

The ecosystem devices that are compatible with Matter is largely growing. Many companies are making their existing devices compatible with Matter via software updates or new hardware versions.

We expect to see a lot more devices to be available with Matter in the coming years or even months.

Matter is compatible with Apple and Google Home, Home Assistant and Smarthings (Samsungs ecosystem).
