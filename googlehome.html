What is Google Home?

Google Home is practically like HomeKit but just for Android and Google users. You can download the Google Home app on iPhone if that is your preffered system as well.

You can buy hubs for your Google Home by getting one of Google's Nest product line. They make diferent hubs like ones that are affordable and ones that are with screens like the Nest Hub (2nd gen). These hubs also double as speakers for your favorite music or radio station!

You can connect Wifi devices to your Google Home by signing in to a compatible devices account and linking it to your Google Home.
