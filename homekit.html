What is HomeKit?

HomeKit is a smart home controller and an ecosystem of devices on it's own.

The HomeKit ecosystem is made by Apple and there are two main controllers that you would have to get: The HomePod Mini or the Homepod (2nd gen). You can also get an Apple TV and that would also act as a hub.

There is a wide range of devices for you to use with HomeKit. The HomePods are both equipped with Thread radios, so you can use the same (but different) low power deviced and sensors like Zigbee.

To every smart home system you can connect hubs, which are made by different manufacturers, to connect their devices which do not work straight up with HomeKit or some other system.
