What is Amazon Alexa?

Amazon Alexa is a voice assistant known as Alexa. To use Alexa you have to download the Amazon Alexa App. You can launch Alexa in the app, or you can buy an Echo device like the Echo Dot (3rd gen) which is the cheapest on the market currently.

Alexa is a bit like Google Home where you only have to link your accont to Alexa and you connect your devices via Wi-Fi.

There is a gigantic range of devices that you can connect and control only with your voice.

You can also connect different streaming services like Spotify or Pandora to play music. TuneIn (Radio) comes for free pre-installed, so if you don't have the services like Spotify you can still listen to your favorite radio station.
