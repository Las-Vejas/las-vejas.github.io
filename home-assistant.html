What is Home Assistant?

To use Home Assistant you have to run it on a Raspberry Pi 4, or you can use an old computer but, the more storage the better.

Home Assistant can be a frontend and a backend for your smart home. It is very useful in cases where you want to make complicated automations to execute complex actions.

You can make a custom frontend dashboard to amplify your experience of using a smart home app. You can fully customize how your dashboars looks and feels. You can find more infromation here.

To connect devices to your setup you can buy something like a Zigbee dongle to use Zigbee devices without having having to buy an expensive hub.
